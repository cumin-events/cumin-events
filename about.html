About

Originating from Nairobi Kenya, the Anand family arrived to England in 1975; they brought with them a strong palette of Indian taste, traditional flavours as well as a successful history of restaurants and catering. The Anand family rapidly spread across the country opening Restaurants such as Madhus in London.


Today the Anand Empire caters for some of the most exciting events hosted in the most exclusive venues. With our years of experience, knowledge and an in depth understanding of Punjabi cuisine, Cumin Events guarantees a truly unique event designed to astonish your guests. Adhering to an ethos of quality we ensure only the finest ingredients are selected and traditional cooking methods are maintained for the most intense flavour.


The Cumin Events caters for all tastes incorporating both a traditional and contemporary fusion style for our dishes. We guarantee a high quality of service; delicious beautifully presented dishes, which are served by our professionally trained banqueting team. Our event managers will ensure that all your requirements are met and that your day runs effortlessly, so you can enjoy the day stress free. If you trust our years of knowledge in Indian cuisine and service then we are certain to make your special day a truly memorable experience.
