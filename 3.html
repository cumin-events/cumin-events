East Midlands Conference Centre & Orchard Hotel

Hidden away within University Park is the East Midlands Conference Center. Set in 330 acres of landscaped parkland, the EMCC provides a beautiful backdrop to your day. Not only does it boast a banqueting suite that can hold up to 500, adjacent to the East Midlands Conference Center is the Orchard Hotel. Opened in November 2012, this 202-bed Eco-friendly hotel has been designed to maximize the use of natural daylight and features rooftop terraces, floor-to-ceiling windows and unique contemporary decor throughout.

Specifications

  • Up to 500 banquet style
  • Starcloth backdrop
  • Threate room (from religious ceremonies)
  • Orchard Hotel (on site hotel)
  • 202 bedrooms
  • Parking
