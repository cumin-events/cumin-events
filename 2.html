Raddisson Blu Hotel, East Midlands Airport

The Raddisson Blue Hotel is one of Nottingham’s finest venues, over 700 square meters of meeting space on the ground floor with access for disabled guests and Free high-speed, wireless Internet access throughout the hotel. There is a central conference lobby area of 200 square metres, which links all of the rooms and makes the entire area versatile. There are 9 meeting rooms, which are suitable for all occasions.

Specifications

  • A total of 9 meeting rooms
  • A total of 218 bedrooms
  • Convenient location just under 1 mile from East Midlands Airport
  • Free high-speed, wireless Internet access through 500 MB fibre optic
  • On-site car parking for 350 vehicles
