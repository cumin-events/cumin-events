The Belfry Nottingham

The Nottingham Belfry is a stunning wedding venue in Nottingham providing a chic, contemporary backdrop to your special day. The admiralty suite provides the perfect setting for your wedding. Size is no concern, providing you with a space that is stylish and beautiful. This 4 star hotel provides the venue and the bedrooms to provide you with a full complete package.

Specifications

  • Up to 550 banquet style
  • Bar area
  • 20 bedrooms
  • Parking
